About GymBro by Rohit Negi:

Our Story:

GymBro by Rohit Negi started as a passion project in 2023, born from Rohit's own journey from casual lifter to dedicated trainer, aiming to create a no-nonsense space where anyone can build strength and confidence without the intimidation.

What began in a small garage setup has grown into a full-fledged gym, focusing on community-driven fitness that emphasizes real results through hard work, expert guidance, and a supportive bro culture.

We're not just about weights and workouts—we're about transforming lives, fostering friendships, and making fitness a fun, integral part of your routine.

Located in the heart of the city, GymBro serves everyone from beginners to pros, with modern equipment and programs designed for sustainable progress.

Our Mission And Values:

At GymBro, our mission is to empower individuals to unlock their potential through accessible, effective training that builds both body and mind in a judgment-free environment.

We value grit, community, and growth—prioritizing personalized plans, inclusive vibes, and continuous improvement to help you achieve goals like never before.

Sustainability is key: we promote balanced routines that prevent burnout, blending intense sessions with recovery-focused classes for long-term success.

Integrity drives us—every program is backed by certified expertise, ensuring safe, science-based fitness that delivers visible results without gimmicks.
